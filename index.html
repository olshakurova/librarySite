Clayton-Liberty Township Public Library

Welcome to Clayton-Liberty Township Public Library!

Our mission is to provide the means by which people of all ages, interests and circumstances may avail themselves of the recorded wisdom, experiences and ideas of others.

In support of this mission, we assembled and organized materials and made them accessible to all.

We offer opportunities for personal, educational cultural and recreational enrichment.

We place special emphasis on supporting student's needs at all academic levels and on stimulating young children's interest and appreciation for reading and learning.
