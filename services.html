Reserving Materials

Patrons may reserve materials that are currently checked out or on order from the publisher by filling out a “reserve slip” available at the front desk.

Patrons will be notified when the item is available.

Reserve materials will be held at the front desk for 3 days.

Photocopies and FAX

One-sided and two-sided copies are $0 .15 per page.

Copies printed from a website are $.20.

FAX service is available for a charge of $1.00 per page for a local FAX $3.00 per page for a long distance FAX.

Library can provide a generic cover sheet for $0.25. If requested, Library can receive Faxes for patrons at a rate of $0.25 per page.

Community Meeting Room

The Library has a meeting room available for public use.

User fees apply.

Inquire at the front desk or call the library.

Talking Books Service

Audio books are available to patrons through the Indiana State Library for those who qualify for the service.

CLTPL will assist patrons in getting information about this valuable program.

Homebound Delivery Service

CLTPL will assist patrons in securing reading materials should they be unable to come to the library due to illness, infirmities, etc.

Staff may select materials for patrons as time permits.

Staff to be compensated for mileage for delivering library materials – current federal mileage rates apply.
