Library Card

Resident Card
Free to township residents with proof of address.
The Library is supported through residents’ payments of income and property taxes.
Gifts, fines and fees provide additional revenue.
Non-Residing "Resident" Card
Persons owning real estate or other property in Clayton and or Liberty Township but do not live in the library district are also eligible for a free Resident Card.
Tax receipt needed for proof.
Juvenile Card
Juvenile Card is available for a minor (under 18) requesting a Resident Card.
Child must be able to print legibly his/her own name.
A parent/guardian signature is required for a minor’s card.
Juvenile Non-Resident Card or Student Card
Students in the Mill Creek School Corporation, who reside outside of Liberty Township, are exempt from the annual Non-Resident Card Fee.
The Student Card expires annually in Sept. and is only valid for the named student’s use.
Non-Served Card
Patrons who reside in an area of the state that does not have a public library may buy a membership in any Indiana public library.
Annual fees are based on the taxes a resident pays to support the library.
The card is issued in one family member’s name. Additional family member cards are available for $1.00 each.

Additional Borrowing Options

Pubic Library Access Card (PLAC)
This card allows access to any public library in Indiana. Application is available to any resident or non-resident cardholder. The Indiana State Library sets the annual PLAC fee.
Reciprocal Borrowing
We honor a borrower’s card issued by any of the Hendricks County Libraries. We reserved the right to check a patron’s status with his home library and may decline service if patron is not in good standing.

Loan Periods

Books
Books check out for a 2 weeks loan period.
Renewals must be requested on or before due date. May be renewed in person or by phone for an additional two-week period. 1 renewal allowed unless there is a waiting list.
Book limit is 10 to a borrower at any one time.
Magazines
Magazines circulate for 1 week with no renewals.
Limit is 5 to a borrower at any one time.
Video and Audio Materials
Video and Audio Materials circulate for 1 week, no renewals allowed.
Limit of 2 materials per adult card holder.

Fines & Fees

Fines are charged per day per item returned after the due date, including the day returned.

Overdue fines are $0.10 per day per item for both magazines and books.

No fined levied for Sundays, day(s) library is closed, or holidays.

Patrons with unpaid fines/ fees in excess of $1.00 or have materials out and overdue may not check out additional items.

Lost or damaged materials
There is a $1.00 fee accessed for a missing magazine-return envelope.
A lost and /or non-repairable item fee will be the list price to replace the materials. Out-of-Print materials will be accessed a flat fee based on the type of item.
